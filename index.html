About Reina

Hi! I’m a UI/UX designer based in San Diego. I started the Ship It In 14 project to push myself to improve my design skills as well as (finally) learn how to code my own designs. The project was inspired by the podcast, “Product People” by Justin Jackson, as well as a design/coder YouTuber, Mckenzie Child. I wanted to do something similar, where I challenge myself to create and learn continously as well to inspire other creatives to take on their own challenges.  Follow along on my journey! If you are currently working on a project, I’d love to hear about it - get in touch!
